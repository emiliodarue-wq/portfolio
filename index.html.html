How I work

From notebook to screen, in four steps

No site starts with code. This is the path every project follows, from the first sketch to launch day.

01 — Sketch

Understand the project

We talk about your business, who's going to visit the site, and what the page needs to achieve.

02 — Design

Define the style

I propose a palette, typography, and structure before writing any code, with your input at every step.

03 — Build

Build it from scratch

I code the site from the ground up, responsive and fast, with no generic templates involved.

04 — Launch

Ship and fine-tune

We review it together across devices and make the final tweaks before it goes live.
